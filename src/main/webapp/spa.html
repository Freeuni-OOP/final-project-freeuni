Where the city disappears

The OOP Spa spans two floors of the original 1924 building. Stone walls, warm lighting, and a team of specialist therapists create an environment built entirely around rest. Whether you come for an hour or an afternoon, every visit is tailored to you.

Open daily from 09:00 to 21:00. Bookings recommended 24 hours in advance.

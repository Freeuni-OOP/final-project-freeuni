Kazbegi & Gergeti Trinity Church

Travel deep into the Greater Caucasus mountains to the legendary Kazbegi region. Visit the Gergeti Trinity Church perched at 2,170 m with stunning views of Mount Kazbek (5,047 m). Walk through Stepantsminda village, cross the Terek River, and explore medieval stone towers.

  • 🏔 Gergeti Trinity Church (14th century)
  • ⛰ Views of snow-capped Mount Kazbek

Gomis Mta — Above the Clouds

Gomis Mta rises to over 2,100 metres in the Racha region, and on a clear day the view stretches endlessly above an ocean of clouds. This overnight camping tour includes a guided hike through alpine meadows, a campfire dinner, and the unforgettable experience of waking above the clouds at sunrise.

Highlights

  • ⛅ Above-the-cloud panoramic views

Tusheti — Ancient Towers & Wild Mountains

Tusheti is one of the most remote and breathtaking regions in Georgia — a UNESCO heritage landscape of medieval watchtowers, ancient villages, and untouched alpine wilderness. Accessible only by the legendary Abano Pass road (one of the most dramatic drives in the world), this is a journey for the adventurous.

Highlights

  • 🗼 Medieval defensive towers of Omalo
  • 🛤 Abano Pass (2,926 m) — legendary mountain road
  • 🏡 Stay in traditional Tushetian guesthouse
  • 🦅 Eagle-eye aerial views of mountain villages
  • ⚔️ Ancient fortress ruins exploration
  • 🐑 Meet local shepherds and their flocks
⏱

Kass Land — Georgia's Adventure Park

Kass Land is Georgia's largest and most spectacular attraction park, featuring the record-breaking Diamond Bridge zipline, massive rainbow slides, go-kart racing tracks, cable cars, and dozens of activities for all ages. Set in stunning natural surroundings near Gori, this is a full day of pure exhilaration.

Highlights

  • 💎 Diamond Bridge — Guinness World Record zipline
  • 🌈 Massive rainbow tubing slides
  • 🏎 Go-kart racing circuit
  • 🚡 Panoramic cable car rides
  • 🎢 Roller coasters & amusement rides
  • 🍴 Multiple dining options on-site

Mtatsminda — Tbilisi from Above

Take the historic Mtatsminda funicular to the top of Tbilisi's iconic hilltop park. At the summit you'll find panoramic views of the entire city, an amusement park with roller coasters and a ferris wheel, the famous Mtatsminda restaurant, and peaceful walking paths among the trees. A perfect evening outing.

Highlights

  • 🚋 Ride the historic Mtatsminda funicular
  • 🌆 360° panoramic views over Tbilisi

Free University of Tbilisi — A Symbol of Excellence

The Free University of Tbilisi, housed on the stunning Kakha Bendukidze Campus, is home to Georgia's brightest students and most innovative minds. This guided campus visit lets you walk through the modern facilities, hear about Georgia's educational renaissance, enjoy breathtaking views over Tbilisi from the hilltop campus, and witness the energy of a university that has become a symbol of the country's future.

Highlights

  • 🎓 Guided campus tour (Kakha Bendukidze Campus)
  • 🏛 Architecture & design walk
  • 🌆 Panoramic views over Tbilisi valley

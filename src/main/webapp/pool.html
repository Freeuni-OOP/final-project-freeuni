Important notices

⚠️

Capacity limits enforced

Each pool has a strict maximum occupancy. Once full, no additional guests may enter until others leave. We recommend booking 24 hours in advance.

🧒

Children under 12

Children must be accompanied by an adult (18+) at all times. Children under 12 are only permitted in the Kids' Splash Pool and Leisure Pool.

👙

Swimwear required

Proper swimwear is mandatory. Board shorts are permitted in the leisure pool only. Swim caps are required in the Olympic lane pool.

🔄

Cancellation policy

Free cancellation up to 2 hours before your session. No-shows are charged in full. You will receive an email reminder 1 hour before your booking.

Reserve your slot

Sessions last 90 minutes. You will receive a confirmation email with your entry time and pool number. Arrive 10 minutes early to allow time for changing.

Daily session times

  • Morning: 07:00 / 08:30 / 10:00
  • Afternoon: 12:00 / 13:30 / 15:00
  • Evening: 17:00 / 18:30 / 20:00
